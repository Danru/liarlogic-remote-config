Liar Logic Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 May 2026

Liar Logic is a detective puzzle game. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected when you use the app, how it is used, and what choices you have.

Information We Collect

Liar Logic does not require you to create an account and does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, address, or other direct personal details.

The app may collect or process the following types of information:

  • Purchase history: Used to unlock in-app purchases such as Remove Ads, Premium Case Pack 01, and Detective Bundle.
  • Product interaction data: Used to understand how users interact with game features, store items, cases, and app screens.
  • Advertising data and device identifiers: Used by advertising services to display ads, measure ad performance, prevent fraud, and support ad delivery.
  • Crash and performance data: Used to identify crashes, improve stability, and improve app performance.
  • Game progress: Used to save your progress, solved cases, coins, hints, and streaks.

How We Use Information

Information is used to provide app functionality, unlock purchases, show optional rewarded ads, show advertising, improve app stability, measure app performance, and improve the game experience.

Advertising

Liar Logic uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob and related Google services may collect information such as device identifiers, advertising data, app interaction data, and approximate usage information for ad delivery, ad measurement, fraud prevention, and related advertising purposes.

In regions where consent is required, the app may show a consent prompt before certain advertising features are used.

In-App Purchases

Liar Logic uses Apple StoreKit for in-app purchases. Purchase processing is handled by Apple. The app uses purchase status to unlock paid features and restore purchases.

Game Progress

Game progress such as solved cases, coins, hints, and streaks may be stored locally on your device. This allows the app to remember your progress between sessions.

Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services, including Google AdMob and Apple StoreKit. These services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

Data Linked to You

Some data, such as device identifiers, advertising data, purchase history, product interaction data, crash data, and performance data, may be linked to your device or app usage depending on how Apple, Google, or other service providers process it.

Tracking

If the app uses advertising identifiers or advertising services for tracking as defined by Apple, the app will request permission where required by iOS.

Children

Liar Logic is not intended to collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us.

Your Choices

You can manage app permissions through iOS Settings. You can also manage advertising tracking permissions through iOS privacy settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
