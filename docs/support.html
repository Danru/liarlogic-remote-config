In-App Purchases

Liar Logic includes optional in-app purchases such as Remove Ads, Premium Case Pack 01, and Detective Bundle.

If a purchase does not appear after buying, open the app, go to the Store screen, and tap Restore Purchases.

Ads and Rewarded Ads

The app may show interstitial ads and optional rewarded ads. Rewarded ads are only used when you choose to watch an ad for an in-game reward.

If you bought Remove Ads, standard interstitial ads should no longer appear. Rewarded ad options may be replaced by coin-based options where appropriate.

Premium Case Pack 01

Premium Case Pack 01 unlocks additional detective cases. If the pack remains locked after purchase, use Restore Purchases from the Store screen.

Gameplay Help

Each case asks you to read the clues, compare witness statements and evidence, then identify the liar and the clue that proves the lie.
